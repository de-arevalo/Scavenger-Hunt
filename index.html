Iron Man 3 Scavenger Hunt

You are Tony Stark during the Iron Man 3 movie. After being attacked by the Mandarin, Jarvis sends you to Tennessee in your Mark 42 suit. After crashing into snow and destroying your suit, you begin to wander off. Your mission is to find your desperately needed treasure.

Double click to begin.

Move your mouse to the ground to start walking.

You found a restaurant that set out a poncho! Perfect to keep you warm during your search. But the doors are locked, so you should find another place to stay. Click to go back.

You decide to head a different way. Click to keep walking.

A shed! But someone's already inside. Click the button to hide behind a tree until they leave.

After waiting a while, you think the coast is clear. Double click the button again to stop hiding and check.

All clear. Time to go inside. Click the doors of the shed.

Woohoo! You found the treasure: a warm place to stay the night and recharge Mark 42. Congrats.
